Help & Instructions

Welcome to the Scrum Board App! This tool is designed to help you manage your projects using the Scrum framework effectively. Below are the instructions to guide you through the main features of the app. To access the Scrum Board, you must log in with your credentials. If you do not have an account, please register by clicking the "Register" link in the navigation bar. After logging in, you will be redirected to the Board Page, where you can navigate to different sections of the app using the navigation bar at the top.

  • Home: Brings you back to the Home Page.
  • Board: Where you can see the Scrum Board with all the tasks.
  • Manage Users: (Admin Only) Where you can manage user accounts and permissions.
  • Account: View and edit your account details.
  • Logout: Safely exit your account.

The Scrum Board is divided into several columns, each representing a different stage of task completion: Backlog, Ready, In Progress, Review, and Done.

Adding Tasks
  1. Click on the Add Task button.
  2. Fill in the task details such as title, description, and estimated hours.
  3. Assign the task to a team member (if applicable).
  4. Click Submit to add the task to the board.
Editing Tasks
  1. Click on a task to open its details.
  2. Click the Edit button to modify the task's title, description, status, or assigned user.
  3. After making changes, click Save to update the task.
Deleting Tasks

Click the Delete button to remove a task from the board.

You can change your password or update your profile picture by navigating to the Account page. Ensure your new password is strong and confirm it before saving.

Managing Users

Go to the Manage Users section to add new users, change user rights, reset passwords, or delete users.

User Rights

Change Rights: Adjust user permissions according to their role.

Reset Password: Set a new password for a user if they forget theirs.

Delete User: Remove a user from the system.

If you require further assistance or have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact us.
